BOOKING APP

This is a train transportation booking app that allows users to book train tickets with ease. The application is designed to streamline the ticket reservation process by providing a simple and intuitive interface for both new and returning users. With this app, users can:

  1. Log in to access their bookings.
  2. Book a ticket by entering passenger details and confirming their trip.
