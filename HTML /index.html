BOOKING APP

This is a train transportation booking app that allows users to search, view, and book train tickets with ease. The application is designed to streamline the ticket reservation process by providing a simple and intuitive interface for both new and returning users. With this app, users can:

  • Create an account or log in to access their bookings.
  • Search available trains by entering departure and destination stations along with a travel date.
  • Book a ticket by entering passenger details and confirming their trip.
